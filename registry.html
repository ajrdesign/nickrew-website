Registry

We know that many people love to give gifts at weddings, so we’ve put together a few small registries for you to look through.

Wanderable

We are including wanderable, which is not your typical wedding registry where you can send gifts of the non-traditional variety, because back in 2011 Nicki’s Grandma Lois passed away, and left her with almost anything a person would need or want to start a home. Thanks to Lois, and the fact that we have been together for nearly three years, there’s very little that we need in terms of typical household necessities. So with wanderable we are able to put together a different kind of registry.

Anthropologie

At this time, Anthropologie only allows online purchases to be deducted from our Wish List registry. Any items purchased in-store will remain on the Wish List registry until we remove the item ourselves. This means if you purchase something in-store, feel free to send us a note and have us remove the item from the Wish List registry.
